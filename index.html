Mission in Portugal

I served a 2 year long mission for The Church of Jesus Christ of latter-day Saints throughout the country of Portugal. During this blessed period of my life I was able to serve others while teaching them about our loving Savior Jesus Christ. I had countless conversations with hundreds of people all with unique life stories from all different backgrounds. While teaching others I also deepened my own faith in Christ and saw many miracles work in my life and the lives of those around me.
